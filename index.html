Instructions

  1. Allow the system to use your webcam.
  2. Type in the name of the person you want the system to recognize.
  3. Have that person's face fully visible in front of the camera and press "Train class". Note that the first time you activate your system there might have to wait for a few seconds. You'll know it started when boxes appear on your face.
  4. You may now detect this person by pressing "Single shot" or "Real-time".

Note that while the system can detect multiple faces at once, you must train for each face separately for it to run properly.

Note also that all this data is stored in your browser and is not accessible to me or other third parties. So go bonkers with it!
